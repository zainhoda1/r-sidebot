You can use this sidebar to filter and sort the data based on the columns available in the `dinosaur` table. Here are some examples of the kinds of questions you can ask me:

1. Filtering: "Show only female Velociraptors from the Northern Plains."
2. Sorting: "Show all data sorted by speed_kph in descending order."
3. Answer questions about the data: "How does weight compare between species?"

Available columns: species, habitat, weight_kg, bill_length_cm, speed_kph, claw_length_cm, sex.

You can also say "Reset" to clear the current filter/sort, or "Help" for more usage tips.
